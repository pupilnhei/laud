Our Story

"From chance encounters to lifelong commitment, our journey together has been filled with laughter, adventures, and growth. We're excited to begin this new chapter in the mountains we both love."

Our paths crossed unexpectedly at a friend's birthday party five years ago. What started as casual conversation quickly blossomed into something more meaningful as we discovered our shared love for mountain treks, quiet evenings with good books, and passionate debates about the best coffee spots in town.

The mountains have always held a special place in our hearts. It was during a trek in Uttarakhand, with the majestic Himalayas as our witness, that we realized this was more than just companionship. Our first kiss happened under starlit skies, surrounded by the whispers of pine trees and the distant melody of a mountain stream.

After years of dating, countless adventures, and navigating life's ups and downs together, we knew we wanted to build a life alongside each other. Now, we're returning to the mountains that brought us together to exchange our vows and begin our journey as partners for life. We invite you to join us in celebrating not just our wedding day, but the beautiful journey that has led us here.
